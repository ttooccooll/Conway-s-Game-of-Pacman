How to Play

Conway's Game of Life controls the walls:

  • Any live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbors survives.
  • Any dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes alive.
  • All other cells die or remain dead.
  • I'll also throw in the occasional glider or spaceship. These also form naturally, but I'm going to break the rules here so the board never gets boring.

After that, it's pacman! ...but with six ghosts, each with their own hunting style...who are always invincible...and the coins you pickup are bitcoin...but not real bitcoin...also the walls will kill you

The arena wraps around: run off one edge and you appear on the opposite side. The ghosts and the walls know this trick too.

The game speeds up as your score climbs, and gliders show up more often the longer you survive. When you die, you can continue right where you fell — starting at 121 sats and doubling each time. Keeping your score alive costs more than a fresh game, on purpose.

You get one free game per day. Additional games require Bitcoin Lightning payments. Don't get shocked! Too noisy? The 🔊 button up top mutes everything.

Got an Alby Hub or another NWC wallet? Open stats → Connect wallet and paste your connection secret — plays, continues, and zaps become one tap. Totally optional: QR and extension payments keep working as always.

Login with ANYONE's npub. Just know, the zaps are real. They won't go to you if you use someone else's npub. Hey, maybe play as an organization that you want to support.

When it's all over, brag about it: share your final score straight to Nostr from the game-over screen (uses your browser extension, or copies the text if you don't have one).
